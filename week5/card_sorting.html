My Card Sorting

     The sorting that was chosen differs from the NASA Earth's Moon website in many ways; however, keep in
  mind that this is the result of multiple people's sorting combined into one agreeable final sort. The names of
  the links on the left toolbar did not change, but the content within the links did change and/or were moved
  around. Both the sorters and I agreed that subjects such as opportunities for working alongside NASA and   NASA's partners should be combined because of the similarities of becoming/already being affiliated with
  this organization. The other changes were made because they added more basic simplicity to the organization
  of the content on the website. It was difficult to arrange the information and content of this website because
  none of us were familiar with any detailed facts or the deep history of our planet's moon.
