My Background

      I am an extremely big country music fan and it is by far my favorite genre of music.  Some of my favorite country artists   include: George Strait, Zac Brown Band, Jake Owen, Brad Paisley, Dierks Bentley, Billy Currington, and many more.  I
  have been a fan of country music for as long as I can remember and I doubt I'll ever stop listening to this genre.  I find that
  I can relate to the lyrics in country music more than any other genre of music, i.e. Rap, because of the type of town
  Carson City, MI is.  My passion for country music is the reason that I am creating this website.  There are countless other   genres of music that have websites which allow its users access to a constantly updated list of Top 100 songs, New   Songs, Music Videos, etc.  I would like to create a flawless website that does all of those things for country music.

My Website

      I would like to create a website called www.CountryAllDay.com which will feature all of those catagories that I mentioned   above.  My website will allow it's users to browse the current Top 100 country songs/albums, the most   popular/latest/upcoming New Songs the day they are released to the public and the most popular and latest Music Videos.   When users create an account, they will have the option to pay a contract-free monthly fee to have unlimited music and   video download access to all of the website's content; however, users can share music content on the website to others
  at no charge.  Users will also have the option to create a list of their favorite country artists so as soon as a user's favorite   artists on the list releases a new song, album or music video, they will receieve a notification next time they sign into their   account.  There will also be an free option for the user to receieve an e-mail or text message updating them that they can   instantly access the new music released.
